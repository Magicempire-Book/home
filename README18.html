üëΩUpgrade Items

There are three different potions for card upgrades:Strength\Defense Potion、 EXP Potion、 TURN Potion.

Strength\Defense:Each bottle of potion increases attack power and defense power randomly.

EXP Potion:Each bottle of potion provides 10 EXP points.

TURN Potion：Each potion increases the number of rounds by one point.
