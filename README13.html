🦠Skill Card collection

Magic Empire is unique in that it supports players' skill cards in all game modes. Each type of skill card represents a different additional function, and can be used for cards with no time limit.

A total of 900 skill cards (800 random Acid Rain, Meteo, Castle, Crumbling Tower, and 100 mysterious) are circulated in the game system through public sale. Players can exchange all gems through the market.

Acid Rain Card

Added player card power statistics in PvP and PvE modes.

Meteo Card

In PvP, PvE mode, the EXP gained by the card of the winning player +10%.

Castle Card

In PvP, PvE mode, the Bonus reward of the winning player +10%.

Thunderbolt Card

Increase the defense statistics of player cards in PvP and PvE modes.

Mysterious gems-20 jewels

Includes 4 abilities of 4 gems: Acid Rain Card, Meteo Card, Castle Card and Thunderbolt Card.

Fixed 10% increase in benefit.

Obtainable through Hero Stone.
